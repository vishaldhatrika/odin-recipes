Chicken Masala

Chicken Masala, also known as Chicken Tikka Masala, is a beloved Indian dish renowned for its rich flavors and aromatic spices. Originating from the Indian subcontinent, this dish features tender pieces of chicken marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, typically including garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder.

The marinated chicken is then grilled or roasted to perfection before being simmered in a luscious tomato-based sauce enriched with cream or yogurt. The result is a tantalizingly creamy and spicy dish with a perfect balance of heat and tanginess. Chicken Masala is often served with fluffy basmati rice or warm naan bread, making it a comforting and satisfying meal that's cherished by food enthusiasts worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green Chilies
  • Yogurt
  • Spices
  • Oil
  • Coriander Leaves

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, salt, turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add the marinated chicken.
  3. Cook the chicken until it is half done.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.
  5. In the same pan, add more oil and add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies.
  6. Cook until the onions are translucent.
  7. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft.
  8. Blend the onion-tomato mixture into a smooth paste.
  9. Return the paste to the pan and add the half-cooked chicken.
  10. Cook until the chicken is done.
  11. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
